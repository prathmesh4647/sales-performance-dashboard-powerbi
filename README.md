# Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance across product categories, customer segments, and regions. The dashboard provides valuable business insights through KPI tracking, trend analysis, and interactive visualizations.

## Key Features

* KPI Cards for Sales, Profit, Quantity Sold, and Profit Margin
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales by Product Category
* Customer Segment Analysis
* Regional Profit Analysis
* Interactive Region, Category, and Customer Segment Filters
* Detailed Sales Summary Matrix

## Dashboard Insights

### KPI Monitoring

Track important business metrics:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Quantity Sold
* Profit Margin %

### Monthly Sales Trend

Analyze sales performance over different months and identify growth patterns.

### Product Category Analysis

Compare sales performance across:

* Electronics
* Furniture
* Office Supplies

### Customer Segment Analysis

Understand contribution from:

* Consumer
* Corporate
* Small Business

### Regional Performance

Evaluate profit generated across:

* East
* West
* North
* South
